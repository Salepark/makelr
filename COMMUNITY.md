## Our Philosophy

Makelr is not just a product. It is a **tool-building ecosystem**.

We believe in:

- Transparency over magic
- Ownership over lock-in
- Systems over hacks
- Learning over copy-paste
- Collaboration over gatekeeping

We are building a **workflow-first automation platform**, not a black-box AI toy.

---

## Who This Community Is For

You are welcome here if you are:

- A builder who wants control over automation
- A researcher who cares about repeatable workflows
- A creator who prefers systems over tricks
- A developer who values clarity and architecture
- A user who wants to understand how things work

You do **not** need to be an expert.  
Curiosity and respect matter more than experience.

## How Decisions Are Made

- Small changes: Maintainers decide after review
- Medium changes: Discussed in issues or PRs
- Big changes: Design discussion + community feedback + maintainer decision

We aim for:
- Open discussion
- Clear rationale
- Documented decisions

But we also accept that:
> Not every decision can be perfectly democratic.

## On AI Providers and Data

Makelr is designed around **BYO LLM (Bring Your Own LLM)**.

This means:
- Users control their own API keys
- Users choose their own providers
- Users are responsible for their own data usage and compliance

The project does **not** aim to:
- Spy on user data
- Centralize AI access
- Enforce provider choices
